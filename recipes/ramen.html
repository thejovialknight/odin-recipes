Ramen

Description

This dish is a delicious way to spend time with the family. You can slurp up noodles to your heart's content, baby! You are the slurp king, in particular fact.

Don't waste any damn time, try the recipe right now! It's got good tastes and that's what you want in a food, after all.

Ingredients

  • 150g somen noodles
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • Some spices

Steps

  1. Simmer beef broth with some water
  2. Add spices to the broth
  3. Boil noodles for 3 minutes
  4. Drain noodles, place in bowl with broth
  5. Garnish as desired and eat promptly
